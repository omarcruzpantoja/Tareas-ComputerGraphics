Functions of the demo
At loading a triangle will be drawn at the center of the canvas.

Click inside the circle of any vertex and it will be selected(when selected it will be highlighted in color yellow.

Once a vertex is selected if '-' is pressed, it will delete the selected vertex, if '+' is pressed, a new vertex
will be added in between the selected vertex and the next one in list.

A vertex might be dragged around if selected and dragged.

If a vertex is selected and any arrow key is pressed, it will reposition the selected vertex 10 pixels in to the position selected

To unselect a vertex, click anywhere inside the canvas(outside the range/radius of each vertex)
